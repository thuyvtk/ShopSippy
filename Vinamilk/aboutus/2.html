SHIPPING SERVICES

Can I ship internationally?

Online purchases are restricted for delivery outside U.S. addresses, including Alaska, Hawaii 
and Puerto Rico. We are unable to deliver online purchases to other places at this time, but 
invite our clients to visit a local Gucci boutique. To locate a Gucci boutique near you, please
visit our store locator. To shop online for delivery to another country, select “Ship to: United 
States” located at the top left of your screen. 

International credit cards are subject to authorization. If unable to obtain authorization and 
email will be sent to notify of cancellation. Please use an alternate card card or PayPal to 
complete your purchase.

Can I ship to multiple addresses?

We can only ship to one address per order. To ship to multiple addresses, please place a separate 
order for each location.

How can I track my order?

Shippy will send you several emails during the order process to keep you informed of the status 
of your purchase. 

Availability: 

Products on our site are subject to availability. Once you select a size, a message will indicate 
if that item is available now or available for backorder-pre-order. 

Modification: 

Once an order has been successfully submitted, it cannot be cancelled or modified. 
We advise you to follow the return instructions included in your package.
