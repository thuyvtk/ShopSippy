PAYMENT

Which payment methods do you accept?

Sippy Shop accepts the following forms of payment for online purchases: 
    
(1) Visa 
(2) Mastercard 
(3) Ship Code

Can I use multiple methods of payment?

At this time we only accept one (1) form of payment per order

Will I be charged sales tax?

Sippy Shop charges applicable state, local and county taxes on online transactions. 
Your on-screen total reflects estimated tax. 
The actual sales tax will be calculated when your order is shipped and may vary from 
the estimated tax.
